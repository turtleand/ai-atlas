# SEO Copywriting
**Status:** Submerged

## Why this area is automated
Keyword-optimized content that follows a formula (listicles, product descriptions, meta tags, landing page copy) is exactly what LLMs excel at. The volume of AI-generated SEO content has exploded, making it nearly impossible for human SEO writers to compete on price or speed.

## Timeline
- **2023-06** - Jasper, Copy.ai, and dozens of AI writing tools target SEO content
- **2024-01** - Google updates algorithms to handle AI-generated content flood
- **2024-08** - Freelance SEO writing rates drop 40-60% on platforms like Upwork
- **2025-03** - Most agencies use AI for first drafts of all SEO content
- **2025-10** - Pure SEO copywriting roles are largely eliminated; remaining work is editing and strategy
