# SEO Copywriting
**Status:** Submerged

Keyword-optimized content that follows a formula -- listicles, product descriptions, meta tags, landing page copy -- is exactly what LLMs excel at. The volume of AI-generated SEO content has exploded, making it nearly impossible for human SEO writers to compete on price or speed.

## Latest
- **2025-10** -- Pure SEO copywriting roles are largely eliminated; remaining work is editing and strategy. Source: Industry data
- **2025-03** -- Most agencies use AI for first drafts of all SEO content. Source: Marketing reports

## History
- **2024-08** -- Freelance SEO writing rates drop 40-60% on platforms like Upwork. Source: Upwork data
- **2024-01** -- Google updates algorithms to handle AI-generated content flood. Source: Search Engine Journal
- **2023-06** -- Jasper, Copy.ai, and dozens of AI writing tools target SEO content. Source: Industry data
