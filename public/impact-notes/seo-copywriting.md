# SEO Copywriting
**Status:** Submerged

Keyword-optimized content that follows a formula -- listicles, product descriptions, meta tags, landing page copy -- is exactly what LLMs excel at. The volume of AI-generated SEO content has exploded, making it nearly impossible for human SEO writers to compete on price or speed.

## Latest
- **2026-06-29**: Google's live breaking-news treatment inside AI Overviews shows search moving from ranked links toward an answer and news interface. SEO copywriting pressure shifts from keyword pages toward source credibility, structured evidence, and visibility across AI-mediated discovery. ## History
- **2026-02-22** -- Content autopilot platforms now offer fully hands-off publishing workflows handling research, generation, SEO optimization, CMS publishing, and instant search engine indexing. Sight AI's 13+ specialized AI agents work together without human direction, automatically submitting content to search engines via IndexNow integration the moment it goes live. The platform also tracks AI visibility across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity to monitor brand mentions in AI search results. Other tools like Jasper and Copy.ai offer campaign workflows and custom multi-step automation. The shift moves from "AI writing assistants" requiring constant oversight to true autopilot systems that can publish 100 articles per month with minimal intervention. The difference now is automation depth that eliminates repetitive tasks while maintaining brand standards through AI voice training. Source: [Sight AI](https://www.trysight.ai/blog/content-autopilot-mode-tools)
- **2025-10** -- Pure SEO copywriting roles are largely eliminated; remaining work is editing and strategy. Source: Industry data
- **2025-03** -- Most agencies use AI for first drafts of all SEO content. Source: Marketing reports
- **2024-08** -- Freelance SEO writing rates drop 40-60% on platforms like Upwork. Source: Upwork data
- **2024-01** -- Google updates algorithms to handle AI-generated content flood. Source: Search Engine Journal
- **2023-06** -- Jasper, Copy.ai, and dozens of AI writing tools target SEO content. Source: Industry data
