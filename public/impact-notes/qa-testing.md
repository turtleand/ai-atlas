# QA Testing
**Status:** Frontier

AI can generate test cases, write assertions from specs, and identify flaky tests. Manual QA for straightforward user flows is being automated. But edge cases, exploratory testing, and understanding user intent still need humans.

## Latest
- **2026-02-18** -- Vinod Khosla predicts IT services and BPO firms (which heavily rely on QA testing roles) could "almost completely disappear" by 2030 as AI eliminates large parts of routine white-collar work. Khosla's warning targets the outsourcing industry's reliance on standardized processes.
