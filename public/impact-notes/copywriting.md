# Copywriting
**Status:** Frontier

AI can write competent marketing copy, email campaigns, social media posts, and ad variations. The quality is good enough for many use cases. But brand voice, emotional resonance, and truly creative campaigns still need human writers.

## Latest
- **2025-12** -- Copywriter role evolves: less writing, more directing and editing AI output. Source: Industry trends
- **2025-05** -- AI handles A/B testing copy variations at scale, outperforming humans on conversion metrics. Source: Marketing reports

## History
- **2024-10** -- Freelance copywriting market contracts; surviving writers specialize in voice and strategy. Source: Upwork data
- **2024-04** -- Agencies report 30-50% faster turnaround using AI-assisted workflows. Source: AdAge
- **2023-09** -- ChatGPT becomes a default first-draft tool for copywriters. Source: Industry consensus
