# Copywriting
**Status:** Frontier

AI can write competent marketing copy, email campaigns, social media posts, and ad variations. The quality is good enough for many use cases. But brand voice, emotional resonance, and truly creative campaigns still need human writers.

- **2026-02-17** -- Microsoft AI chief Mustafa Suleyman warns that millions of UK office jobs, including marketing roles, could be fully automated by AI within 2 years. AI is approaching "human-level performance on most professional tasks" including marketing strategies. Senator Bernie Sanders calls widespread automation an "economic earthquake." Source: [The Workers Union](https://www.theworkersunion.com/2026/02/16/ai-could-obliterate-uk-office-jobs-in-under-2-years-microsoft-chief-issues-stark-warning/)
- **2025-12** -- Copywriter role evolves: less writing, more directing and editing AI output. Source: Industry trends
