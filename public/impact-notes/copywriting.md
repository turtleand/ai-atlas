# Copywriting
**Status:** Frontier

## Why this area is shifting
AI can write competent marketing copy, email campaigns, social media posts, and ad variations. The quality is good enough for many use cases. But brand voice, emotional resonance, and truly creative campaigns still need human writers. The frontier is where "good enough" AI copy meets "worth paying for" human craft.

## Timeline
- **2023-09** - ChatGPT becomes a default first-draft tool for copywriters
- **2024-04** - Agencies report 30-50% faster turnaround using AI-assisted workflows
- **2024-10** - Freelance copywriting market contracts; surviving writers specialize in voice and strategy
- **2025-05** - AI handles A/B testing copy variations at scale, outperforming humans on conversion metrics
- **2025-12** - Copywriter role evolves: less writing, more directing and editing AI output
