# Mechanism Design
**Status:** Safe

Mechanism design remains above the waterline because the core problem is choosing incentives, constraints, and governance loops that keep automated financial systems aligned with real-world consequences.

This note records Turtleand's interpreted signals from public news sources. It is not a universal disruption score.

## Latest
- **2026-06-21**: The perps regulation fight shows that crypto mechanism design now includes legal classification, margin rules, liquidation behavior, and market-integrity constraints. The design problem is becoming market-structure governance, not only protocol incentives. Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/06/21/are-perps-swaps-a-quick-look-at-that-cme-suit-state-of-crypto)

- **2026-06-20**: Stablecoin yield, ethics, and DeFi treatment in the CLARITY Act debate show that mechanism design is becoming public-market and policy design.
