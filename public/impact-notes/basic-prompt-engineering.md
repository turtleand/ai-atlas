# Basic Prompt Engineering
**Status:** Frontier

## Why this area is shifting
As models get smarter, the need for elaborate prompting techniques decreases. Simple "prompt engineering" tricks (few-shot examples, chain-of-thought, role-playing) are being absorbed into model capabilities. The models figure out what you mean without needing careful prompt construction.

## Timeline
- **2024-01** - "Prompt engineer" job postings peak, then begin declining
- **2024-07** - Claude and GPT-4 handle complex tasks with minimal prompting
- **2025-01** - Models with built-in reasoning (o1, Claude thinking) reduce need for chain-of-thought prompting
- **2025-08** - Basic prompting knowledge becomes a general skill, not a specialized role
- **2026-01** - Advanced prompt engineering evolves into system design and evaluation
