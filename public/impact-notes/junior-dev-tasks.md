# Junior Dev Tasks
**Status:** Submerged

Tasks traditionally assigned to junior developers -- implementing features from specs, writing unit tests, wiring up components -- are increasingly within reach of AI coding assistants. The role is not gone, but the bar for what a junior needs to bring is rising fast.

## Latest
- **2026-06-18** -- AWS agent tooling and agentic commerce signals suggest routine implementation work is moving closer to production automation, not just assisted coding. Junior work still matters when it builds judgment, but the repeatable spec-to-code layer is now below the June waterline. The company plans to hire senior AI engineers to replace the broader workforce it just let go. This is the clearest example yet of a major tech company explicitly citing AI productivity gains as the reason for mass layoffs, not a downturn.
