# Junior Dev Tasks
**Status:** Frontier

## Why this area is shifting
Tasks traditionally assigned to junior developers, like implementing features from specs, writing unit tests, and wiring up components, are increasingly within reach of AI coding assistants. The role is not gone, but the bar for what a junior needs to bring is rising fast.

## Timeline
- **2024-01** - Tech companies begin hiring fewer junior developers, citing AI productivity gains
- **2024-08** - Y Combinator startups report building MVPs with 1-2 senior devs plus AI tools
- **2025-03** - "AI-native" job postings expect familiarity with coding agents as a baseline skill
- **2025-09** - Debate intensifies about the junior developer pipeline and mentorship gaps
- **2026-01** - Junior roles evolve toward "AI-assisted developer" with emphasis on review and integration
