# Smart Contract Auditing
**Status:** Frontier

## Why this area is shifting
AI tools can scan Solidity and other smart contract code for known vulnerability patterns faster than manual review. Static analysis combined with LLM understanding catches common issues like reentrancy, overflow, and access control flaws. But novel attack vectors, economic exploits, and protocol-level vulnerabilities still need human auditors who understand DeFi mechanics.

## Timeline
- **2024-02** - AI-assisted audit tools (Slither + LLM integration) gain adoption at audit firms
- **2024-08** - Automated auditing catches 70%+ of known vulnerability classes
- **2025-03** - Several audit firms offer "AI-first" audit tiers at lower price points
- **2025-09** - Human auditors focus on economic modeling and novel attack surfaces
- **2026-01** - Standard vulnerability scanning is automated; creative security analysis remains human
