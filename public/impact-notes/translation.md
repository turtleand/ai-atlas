# Translation
**Status:** Submerged

Neural machine translation has reached near-human quality for many language pairs and content types. Business documents, technical manuals, and general content can be translated automatically with minimal editing. Literary translation, cultural adaptation, and specialized domains still need human translators.

## Latest
- **2026-06-18** -- Routine translation moves below the waterline. OS-level AI and local-agent hardware make general translation an ambient feature. Human value remains in cultural judgment, editorial localization, voice, and accountability. Sources: [Apple WWDC 2026, The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/tech/944110/wwdc-2026-news-announcements), [Nvidia RTX Spark AI PCs, HP](https://www.hp.com/us-en/newsroom/press-releases/2026/computex.html)
