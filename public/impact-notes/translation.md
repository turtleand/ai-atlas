# Translation
**Status:** Frontier

## Why this area is shifting
Neural machine translation has reached near-human quality for many language pairs and content types. Business documents, technical manuals, and general content can be translated automatically with minimal editing. Literary translation, cultural adaptation, and specialized domains still need human translators.

## Timeline
- **2023-01** - DeepL and Google Translate improve significantly with LLM-based approaches
- **2024-03** - Real-time voice translation launches in consumer devices (Google Pixel, Apple)
- **2024-09** - Professional translation agencies adopt "post-editing" model: AI translates, humans refine
- **2025-04** - Translation rates drop as turnaround expectations shift from days to hours
- **2025-11** - Specialized and creative translation remains human; general translation is largely automated
