# Stock Photography
**Status:** Submerged

AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) can produce on-demand visuals for any concept in seconds. Generic business photos, abstract backgrounds, and concept illustrations no longer require photographers or stock libraries.

## Latest
- **2025-06** -- Generic stock photography is functionally replaced; niche and editorial photography survives. Source: PetaPixel
- **2024-11** -- Getty Images launches its own AI generator, pivoting the business model. Source: Getty Images

## History
- **2024-03** -- Stock photography revenue declines as companies generate custom images in-house. Source: Industry data
- **2023-09** -- Shutterstock and Adobe integrate AI generation directly into their platforms. Source: Shutterstock, Adobe
- **2023-01** -- Midjourney and DALL-E reach photorealistic quality for common subjects. Source: Tech press
