# Stock Photography
**Status:** Submerged

## Why this area is automated
AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) can produce on-demand visuals for any concept in seconds. Generic business photos, abstract backgrounds, and concept illustrations no longer require photographers or stock libraries.

## Timeline
- **2023-01** - Midjourney and DALL-E reach photorealistic quality for common subjects
- **2023-09** - Shutterstock and Adobe integrate AI generation directly into their platforms
- **2024-03** - Stock photography revenue declines as companies generate custom images in-house
- **2024-11** - Getty Images launches its own AI generator, pivoting the business model
- **2025-06** - Generic stock photography is functionally replaced; niche and editorial photography survives
